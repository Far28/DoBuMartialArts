Our Facilities

At DoBu Martial Arts, we take pride in providing a comprehensive training environment equipped with everything you need to reach your goals. Our facilities are designed with your comfort, safety, and performance in mind.

Large, Matted Martial Arts Area

Our spacious martial arts area is fully matted with high-quality, shock-absorbent flooring. This provides a safe training surface ideal for a range of martial arts, including Jiu-Jitsu, Karate, Judo, and Muay Thai. The large space allows multiple classes to run simultaneously, with ample room for movement, sparring, and practicing techniques in a safe, controlled environment.

Fully-Equipped Gym

Our gym is stocked with a full range of equipment to support all aspects of your fitness journey. From cardio machines (like treadmills, ellipticals, and stationary bikes) to strength training equipment (including free weights, resistance machines, and cable systems), we have everything you need to build endurance, strength, and flexibility. Our gym is accessible to members at all levels, whether you’re looking to supplement your martial arts training or focus on general fitness.

Sauna & Steam Room

We offer both sauna and steam room facilities to help you relax, recover, and rejuvenate after a workout or class. The sauna provides a dry-heat environment to help with muscle relaxation and detoxification, while the steam room offers a moist heat that is excellent for skin health and respiratory benefits. Both are perfect options for unwinding and easing muscle tension, promoting better recovery.

Changing & Shower Facilities

Our clean, well-maintained changing rooms offer secure lockers, spacious changing areas, and shower facilities for your convenience. These amenities allow you to freshen up after your training sessions, with showers stocked with essential toiletries and hair dryers to ensure you leave the gym feeling refreshed. Our changing rooms are regularly sanitized to maintain a high standard of cleanliness and hygiene for all members.
