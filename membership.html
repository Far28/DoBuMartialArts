Membership Options

Basic Membership

This membership includes access to one martial art with two sessions per week. Ideal for beginners or individuals who want to learn at a comfortable pace.

Monthly Fee: £25.00

Intermediate Membership

This option allows members to focus on one martial art with three sessions per week. It’s a great choice for those looking to advance their skills with more regular practice.

Monthly Fee: £35.00

Advanced Membership

Members can participate in any two martial arts with five sessions per week. Perfect for those who want to diversify their training and engage in multiple martial arts disciplines.

Monthly Fee: £45.00

Elite Membership

This membership offers unlimited classes, providing maximum flexibility for enthusiasts who want to attend as many sessions and explore as many martial arts as possible.

Monthly Fee: £60.00

Private Martial Arts Tuition

For those who prefer one-on-one training, private martial arts tuition is available. Each session provides focused attention from our instructors, tailored to individual goals and skill levels.

Fee: £15.00 per hour

Junior Membership

This membership is exclusively for children, granting access to all kids’ martial arts sessions. It’s a fun and engaging way for young ones to learn discipline, respect, and self-defense in a supportive environment.

Monthly Fee: £25.00

Specialist Courses and Fitness Training

Six-Week Beginners' Self-Defence Course

This intensive course is designed for beginners and covers essential self-defense techniques. It includes two 1-hour sessions per week over six weeks, equipping participants with practical skills for personal safety.

Total Course Fee: £180.00

Fitness Room Access

Members can utilize our fully equipped fitness room for a variety of workouts to complement their martial arts training. This option provides flexibility for those who want to focus on fitness at their own pace.

Fee: £6.00 per visit

Personal Fitness Training

Our personal fitness training sessions offer one-on-one guidance from a certified trainer. Tailored to individual goals, these sessions support improved strength, endurance, and overall fitness.

Fee: £35.00 per hour
