Meet Our Trainers

Mauricio Gomez (Gym Owner/Head Martial Arts Coach)

  • Specializes in multi-disciplinary martial arts training, with extensive knowledge across Judo, Jiu-Jitsu, Karate, and Muay Thai.
  • Holds a 4th Dan Blackbelt in Judo, showcasing advanced expertise and a commitment to traditional techniques.
  • 3rd Dan Blackbelt in Jiu-Jitsu, skilled in grappling and ground fighting strategies.
  • 1st Dan Blackbelt in Karate, focusing on striking techniques and traditional forms.
  • Accredited Muay Thai coach with a deep understanding of powerful striking and clinching techniques.

Sarah Nova (Assistant Martial Arts Coach)

  • Holds a prestigious 5th Dan belt in Karate, representing years of dedication to the discipline.
  • Specialized in advanced Karate techniques, including precision striking, self-defense, and kata (formalized sequences of movements).
  • Skilled in teaching all levels, with a focus on fostering confidence and discipline in beginners.
  • Brings a methodical approach to coaching, emphasizing proper form, technique, and the mental aspects of martial arts.

Guy Victory (Assistant Martial arts Coach)

  • 2nd Dan Blackbelt in Jiu-Jitsu, with expertise in grappling, joint locks, and submissions.
  • 1st Dan Blackbelt in Judo, skilled in throws and takedowns, complementing his Jiu-Jitsu experience.
  • Focuses on combining Judo and Jiu-Jitsu for a well-rounded approach to self-defense and competitive martial arts.
  • Passionate about teaching practical applications, helping students develop skills they can use in real-life scenarios.

Morris Davis (Assistant Martial Arts Coach)

  • Accredited Muay Thai coach, specializing in powerful striking techniques, including kicks, punches, and knee strikes.
  • 3rd Dan Blackbelt in Karate, with a strong foundation in self-discipline and traditional martial arts principles.
  • Known for high-intensity training sessions that improve endurance, power, and agility.
  • Encourages a balanced approach, combining the fluidity of Muay Thai with the structure of Karate for effective self-defense skills.

Traci Santiago (Fitness Coach)

  • BSc in Sports Science, providing a solid scientific background in fitness and body mechanics.
  • Qualified in health and nutrition, with a specialization in diet plans that support martial arts training and athletic performance.
  • Expertise in creating strength and conditioning programs tailored to combat athletes, focusing on core stability, strength, and injury prevention.
  • Works closely with martial arts coaches to ensure fitness routines complement the students’ training, enhancing overall performance.

Harpreet Kaur (Fitness Coach)

  • BSc in Physiotherapy and MSc in Sports Science, providing in-depth knowledge of anatomy, biomechanics, and injury rehabilitation.
  • Specializes in recovery programs and injury prevention techniques for martial artists, keeping students safe and resilient.
  • Designs personalized training routines that accommodate physical limitations and promote gradual progression.
  • Emphasizes flexibility, mobility, and proper body mechanics, which are essential for martial arts performance.
